2023 में चीनी ऑनलाइन पाठ्यक्रम प्लेटफार्मों की विशाल सूची

ऑनलाइन शिक्षा चीन में जीवित और अच्छी तरह से है। संयुक्त रूप से, ये प्लेटफॉर्म 100K से अधिक ऑनलाइन पाठ्यक्रमों की पेशकश करते हैं।

रुई माऔर मनोएल कोर्टेस मेंडेज़

The origins of भारी मात्रा में खुले ऑनलाइन पाठ्यक्रम (MOOCs) in China can be traced back to नवम्बर 2011, when 20 पाठ्यक्रम from Chinese universities were made available online through the खुला पाठ्यक्रम platform आईकोर्स.

For years at क्लास सेंट्रल, we’ve been interested in the development of online education in China. You can find our यहाँ विषय पर कवरेज.

इस लेख में, हम चीन में एमओओसी के उद्भव पर चर्चा करते हैं और चीनी भाषा के ऑनलाइन पाठ्यक्रम प्लेटफार्मों की एक व्यापक सूची संकलित करते हैं।

मूल

The first full-fledged MOOC platform in China, XuetangX, was launched in October 2013 by शिघुआ विश्वविद्यालय. Many more similar platforms would promptly follow.

Like दुनिया भर में ऑनलाइन पाठ्यक्रम प्लेटफॉर्म, Chinese MOOC platforms saw a आवेश in both पाठ्यक्रम संख्या और नामांकन in 2020 due to the pandemic.

In 2022 की शुरुआत, the Chinese platforms listed in this article offered close to 70 हजार online courses. That’s about twice as many as in 2020. Now, in 2023 की शुरुआत, we reached a total of about 100K courses, showing that the growth trend perdures.

On March 28, 2022, China’s Ministry of Education announced the “चीन की स्मार्ट शिक्षा” initiative and launched the comprehensive website चीन, bringing together all the MOOC platforms of mainland China.

XuetangX

In October 2013, शिघुआ विश्वविद्यालय along with partner institutions in China की घोषणा की the launch of XuetangX, China’s first MOOC platform. The are close to 7000 पाठ्यक्रम and programs offered on the platform, including live streamings, online degrees, microcredentials, and cohort-based boot camps. XuetangX reached over 80 मिलियन users at its 8th anniversary on October 10, 2021.

The platform raised सीरीज बी फंडिंग, moved away from Open edX and पुनर्निर्माण their website at the beginning of 2020. In April 2020, यह अंतरराष्ट्रीय हो गया and began to offer around 470 free English courses for global learners.

XuetangX पर क्लास सेंट्रल के पिछले कुछ कवरेज यहां दिए गए हैं:

  • XuetangX ने सीरीज B फंडिंग जुटाई, ओपन edX प्लेटफॉर्म से हटी
  • चीन के सबसे बड़े एमओओसी प्लेटफॉर्म अंतरराष्ट्रीय स्तर पर जाते हैं, अंग्रेजी में पाठ्यक्रम शुरू करते हैं
  • XuetangX: चीन के पहले और सबसे बड़े MOOC प्लेटफॉर्म पर एक नजर

चीनी विश्वविद्यालय एमओओसी

Chinese University MOOC, also often called आईकोर्स, is the fruit of a collaboration between the Chinese internet giant NetEase and the original आईकोर्स लर्निंग प्लेटफॉर्म. The MOOC platform offers over 10,000 free university courses, including राष्ट्रीय स्तर पर मान्यता प्राप्त MOOCs. Free digital certificates are automatically available when students pass quizzes and final exam.

झिहुई शू

झिहुई शू, which translates to “Wisdom Tree”, was launched in दिसंबर 2012. It has the particularity of allowing its university partners to share credit-bearing courses with one another, facilitating academic credit mobility across China.

Of the 14,100 courses offered on the platform, 10,500 (that is, 75%) are credit-granting courses only for on-campus students. The platform offers 45 courses with a free digital certificates. And there are also 18 माइक्रोक्रेडेंशियल प्रोग्राम available.

सीएनएमओओसी

In April 2014, Shanghai Jiao Tong University launched सीएनएमओओसी, a online course platform promoting the integration of MOOCs into China’s on-campus higher education system. The platform offers over 3000 courses. About half of them provide free digital certificates.

ज़ू यिन ऑनलाइन

ज़ू यिन ऑनलाइन launched in November 2017. It was developed by The Open University of China and the Chaoxing Group. The purpose of this online course platform is to serve as a “credit bank” for vocational skills, facilitating the transfer of credit and the recognition of prior learning across different universities. The platform offers nearly over 16,000 course with a free digital certificate.

चीनी एमओओसी

चीनी एमओओसी was founded in फरवरी 2015 by Peking University in collaboration with the Alibaba Group. About 150 मुफ्त पाठ्यक्रम with a मुफ्त डिजिटल प्रमाण पत्र are offered on the platform, most of them provided by Peking University.

यूओओसी ऑनलाइन

The यूनिवर्सिटी ओपन ऑनलाइन पाठ्यक्रम (UOOC) platform was founded by Shenzhen University in February 2016, subsuming the preexisting MOOC platform from अप्रैल 2014. The are 1375 free courses on the platform, with the vast majority also offering credit for on-campus students.

झेजियांग प्रांतीय एमओओसी प्लेटफॉर्म

The Zhejiang Institutions of Higher Learning Online Open Course Sharing Platform — or झेजियांग प्रांतीय एमओओसी प्लेटफॉर्म for short — is backed by the Education Department of the province of Zhejiang, south of Shanghai. There are 17,500 courses offered on the platform.

ई Huixue

In December 2015, the Department of Education of the Aihui District, situated in the northwest of China, launched ई हुआक्स्यू. Like Zhejiang’s Provincial MOOC Platform, the purpose of E-Huixue is to serve as a regional repository for online courses. The platform offers more than 2500 MOOCs with a free digital certificate.

सीक्यूओओसी

चूंगचींग यूनिवर्सिटी ओपन ऑनलाइन लर्निंग प्लेटफॉर्म (CQOOC), was established by the Ministry of Education in चूंगचींग (a नगर पालिका in southwest China) in June 2016. Around 4300 courses, mostly from universities and colleges in Chongqing, are offered on the platform.

रोंग यू ज़ू तांग

Rong You Xue Tang is the new name of the former Beijing MOOC Research Association platform. It was founded in 2016 by Renmin University of China together with several Beijing-based universities. Over 210 मुफ्त पाठ्यक्रम are provided on the platform, some of which are क्रेडिट-पात्र courses.

सीखना

सीखना was launched by the online learning company सीखना in May 2015. There are close to 2700 courses provided on the platform.

ICVE MOOC कॉलेज

आईसीवीई was built for professional education by उच्च शिक्षा प्रेस in 2014. There are over 7300 free courses offered on the platform, and over 80% of them provide a free certificate.

आईसीसी

आईसीसी was launched by उच्च शिक्षा प्रेस in 2016. More than 4000 courses from 210 universities are offered on the platform.

एरया

एरया was launched in January 2015 by the Chaoxing Group, an edtech corporation based in Beijing. The MOOC platform offers around 500 free courses in liberal arts from humanities to creative thinking.

Gaoxiaobang

Gaoxiaobang was established by a subsidiary of हुइके समूह, a company focused on the upskilling and reskilling of professionals in tech-related domains. The platform was launched in September 2015, and it currently offers close to 470 free courses.

PMPHMOOC

PMPHMOOC was established in 2015 by the medical publisher पीपुल्स मेडिकल पब्लिशिंग हाउस in partnership with medical schools and academic institutions across China. It provides 70 स्वास्थ्य संबंधी एमओओसी in 10 subjects — for instance, in anatomy and biochemistry.

UMOOCs

UMOOCs is a Chinese MOOC platform for studying foreign languages. The platform was founded by Beijing Foreign Studies University in December 2017, and it is affiliated with the Foreign Language Teaching and Research Press, a publishing company dedicated to all facets of foreign language study in China. It offers over सर्टिफिकेट के साथ 400 फ्री कोर्स covering 10 languages.

आईलैब-एक्स

आईलैब-एक्स is a MOOC platform for virtual simulation experiments launched by उच्च शिक्षा प्रेस in 2017. Close to 3500 virtual experiments are provided on the platform.

एमओईसी

एमओईसी is a MOOC platform for virtual simulation experiment education in medicine, launched by the company मेंगू in 2020. The MOOC platform has provided around 720 courses in medicine for over 100 university medical schools in China.

शिक्षक

शिक्षक is a MOOC platform for computer science and programming that was launched in July 2016. There are over 640 free courses and thousands of free programming exercises. The MOOC platform also allows its verified users to create their own courses, exercises, and ऑनलाइन कक्षाएं.

eWant

eWant is a MOOC platform that was launched by National Chiao Tung University in 2013. National Chiao Tung University is one of Taiwan’s leading public research universities, located in Hsinchu, Taiwan. It hosts more than 3200 courses from over 90 different universities.

शेयरकोर्स

शेयरकोर्स was founded by Taiwan’s National Tsing Hua University in सितंबर 2012. 90 institutions contributed over 650 courses to the MOOC platform. About 190 of them are free.

ओपनएडू

ओपनएडू is a MOOC platform from Taiwan. The MOOC platform is based on Open edX and offers over 470 courses in subjects including computer science, business management, humanities and art.
